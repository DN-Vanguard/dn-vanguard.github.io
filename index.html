About Me

Hi there! My name is Domiltan Nguyen, but everyone calls me Dom! A little about me, I was born and raised in Seattle, WA, I like listening to kpop, playing tennis, and watching anime! I also graduated from the University of Washington with Bachelors in Marketing and IS in Spring of 2020!

After several months of working a marketing job I did not enjoy, I decided to focus on my other degree. However, upon recieving hundreds of rejection emails from various IS related positions, I realized that I lacked experience, and my college coursework was either not enough or applicable to what the companies were looking for. I wanted to find a way to get hands-on experience, and after days of researching, I stumbled upon UW's Coding Bootcamp. After reaching out to UW Coding Bootcamp alums, I learned how remarkable and successful they became after graduating from the bootcamp. Knowing myself, if I put in the work in this bootcamp, it would pay off in the long run, so here I am!

It is NEVER too late to figure out what you want in life. It is your life, so take charge and don't live with regrets.
